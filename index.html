About Us

Laki Café & Pet Hotel in Melaka is inspired by Kiki and Lala, our Pomeranians, and Lily, our Ragdoll cat. We are a plant-friendly, pet-friendly café with separated zones for pet lovers and those who prefer a quiet corner.

Café with pet and non-pet zones — serving delicious food & drinks
Grooming service at 1st floor (LuHouse Pet Salon)
Pet Daycare (11am–8pm)
Pet Hotel with private rooms for dogs & cats + CCTV access
Events & Pet Gatherings

House Rules

To ensure a safe, healthy, and happy environment for everyone.

Laki Pet Hotel & Daycare Rules

  • Fully vaccinated: Required for all pets staying in hotel/daycare.

  • No aggressive pets: For the safety of others.

  • Check-in & Check-out: Check-in is at 11am, check-out is at 8pm (late pick-up may incur extra charges).

Laki Café Rules

  • Pets are welcome in the pet zone only.

  • Clean up after your pets.

  • Be considerate: No excessive barking or disturbance.

  • Respect guests in the non-pet zone who prefer a pet-free dining area.
